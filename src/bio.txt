Dr. Anthony Blaom earned degrees in Mathematics (PhD) and Aeronautics (MSc) from Caltech, California, after graduating top in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Melbourne (BE, Hons). Anthony has been involved in mathematical research for twenty years, publishing on diverse subjects both pure and applied in top mathematical journals. As Lecturer at the University of Auckland (where he still teaches on an adjunct basis), Anthony acquired a reputation as a popular and dedicated teacher.
